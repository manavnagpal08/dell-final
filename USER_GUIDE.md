# Sentinel AI - Complete User Guide

![Map Overview Dashboard](./map_screenshot.png)

Welcome to **Sentinel AI** (Vyom AI)! This platform provides an enterprise-grade digital twin of your infrastructure, augmented by predictive Machine Learning models. This guide will walk you through navigating the dashboard and managing AI alerts.

---

## 📍 1. Understanding the Map Overview

The **Map Overview** is your primary operational interface. It visually represents physical server racks and hardware across your campus in a 3D isometric layout.

### Color Coding (Status Indicators)
- 🟢 **Emerald (Healthy)**: Device is operating within optimal parameters.
- 🟡 **Amber (Warning)**: Minor anomalies detected. AI is monitoring closely.
- 🔴 **Red / Pulsing (Critical)**: Imminent hardware failure predicted. Immediate action required.
- ⚪ **Slate (Offline)**: Device has disconnected from the telemetry network.

### AI Risk Heatmap
At the top right of the Map Overview, you can toggle the **AI Risk Heatmap**.
- **When OFF**: Buildings display their standard operational status.
- **When ON**: The map colors change strictly based on the XGBoost Machine Learning model's calculated `risk_score`. This allows you to spot "invisible" issues before a machine actually enters a Warning state.

---

## 🔍 2. Interacting with Devices

You can click on any server rack or device on the isometric map to open the **Building Details Panel**.

This panel provides:
1. **Live Telemetry**: Health score and real-time Risk percentage.
2. **Hardware Specs**: The specific Vendor, Model, and exact Physical Location.
3. **AI Failure Prediction**: If a failure is predicted, the AI will explicitly state *what* is going to fail (e.g., "Thermal Throttling") and *when* (e.g., "in 3 days").

### Taking Action
At the bottom of the details panel, you have operational buttons:
- **Send Alert**: Broadcasts an emergency notification to the on-site engineering team.
- **Generate Ticket**: Automatically opens a maintenance ticket pre-filled with the AI's diagnostic data.
- **View AI Analysis**: Deep-dive into the specific telemetry curves that led the ML model to its conclusion.

---

## 📈 3. Navigating the Sidebar

The left-hand sidebar gives you access to the broader Sentinel AI ecosystem:

### Executive View
- **Dashboard**: High-level KPI metrics (Total Uptime, Predicted Maintenance Costs, Global Health).
- **Analytics**: Deep-dive historical graphs mapping CPU Load vs. Temperature over the last 30 days.

### Operations
- **Map Overview**: The 3D Digital Twin (Current Page).
- **Alerts**: A chronological ledger of all warnings and critical events generated by the system.

### Hardware Fleet
- **Servers, Network, Cooling**: Filtered lists of your specific hardware types to quickly identify problematic clusters.

---

## 🤖 4. How the AI Works (For Operators)

You don't need to be a Data Scientist to use Sentinel AI. The system handles the complex math behind the scenes.
If you see an **AI Failure Prediction**, it means the system has recognized a pattern in the temperature, power draw, and CPU load that matches previous historical failures. 

**Best Practice**: Never ignore an Amber (Warning) AI Prediction. Generating a maintenance ticket during a Warning phase saves an average of 70% in repair costs compared to waiting for a Critical failure.
